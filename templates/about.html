About Pump Chest

Pump Chest is an innovative AI-powered fitness tracking platform designed to revolutionize your workout experience. Our mission is to provide intelligent, personalized fitness guidance using cutting-edge technology.

Our Vision

We believe that technology can transform fitness tracking from a passive activity to an interactive, intelligent coaching experience. By leveraging AI and computer vision, we help you perfect your form, track your progress, and achieve your fitness goals more effectively.

Key Features

  • Real-time Form Detection
  • Heart Rate Monitoring
  • Personalized Workout Tracking
  • Nutrition and BMI Tracking
  • Advanced Performance Analytics

Our Team

Founded by fitness enthusiasts and tech innovators, Pump Chest combines expertise in machine learning, computer vision, and health sciences to create a unique fitness platform.
